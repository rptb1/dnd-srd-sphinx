Lesser Restoration
------------------

*2nd-level abjuration*

**Casting Time:** 1 action

**Range:** Touch

**Components:** V, S

**Duration:** Instantaneous

.. index::
   single: blinded; cured by lesser restoration
   single: deafened; cured by lesser restoration
   single: paralyzed; cured by lesser restoration
   single: poisoned; cured by lesser restoration
   single: disease; cured by lesser restoration

You touch a creature and can end either one disease or one condition
afflicting it. The condition can be :ref:`blinded`, :ref:`deafened`, :ref:`paralyzed`, or
:ref:`poisoned`.
